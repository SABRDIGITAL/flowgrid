FAQ

Questions people usually ask before they book.

Do you charge a call-out fee?

+

Sometimes. If we do, we’ll say so up front and explain exactly what it covers. For most local leak jobs we fold it into the first hour on site.

Are you insured and Gas Safe registered?

+

Yes. Our engineers are Gas Safe registered for gas work and we carry full public liability insurance. You’ll get certificates after every relevant job.

How quickly can you normally come out?

+

For emergencies we aim for same-day visits where possible. For planned work we agree dates in writing and keep you updated if anything shifts.

What areas do you cover?

+

We’re based in Wiltshire and usually work within roughly a 30–35 minute drive. If you’re not sure, send your postcode and we’ll confirm.
